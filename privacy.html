Privacy Policy for GlintEarn

Last updated: October 2023

Welcome to GlintEarn. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Telegram Mini App.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect minimal information required for the app to function:

  • Telegram User ID & Name: To identify your account and save your balance.
  • Wallet Address: Collected only when you request a withdrawal.
  • IP Address: For security purposes and to prevent multiple accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for:

  • Processing your earnings and withdrawals.
  • Providing customer support through our chat system.
  • Ensuring the security of our platform.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Monetag for advertisements and Firebase for database management. These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures (Firebase security rules and encryption) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the support section in the app or at: glintearnsupport@gmail.com
